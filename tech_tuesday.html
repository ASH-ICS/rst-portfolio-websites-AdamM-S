Blockchains in the modern world A blockchain is a database that is spread on computer networks. They are most commonly known for their important role in cryptocurrency. Block chains are able to maintain secure and decentralized records of transactions. Blockchains are not only limited to cryptocurrencies but they can also be used to make data in any industry unable to be altered. Not all blockchains are completely impenetrable, they can be exploited through coding. Hackers are able to run nodes on blockchains if they want to alter the blockchain and steal cryptocurrency. All they have to do is convince the other nodes that their copy is valid. The hacker needs to control the majority of the network and insert it at the exact moment. This is known as the 51% attack because you need to control more than 50% of a network to attempt it. Blockchains were introduced in 1991, to implement a system where document timestamps could not be tampered with. It was not until almost two decades later, with the launch of Bitcoin in January 2009, that blockchain had its first real-world application. Blockchain is a reliable way of storing data about other types of transactions. All in all, blockchains are well known in today's day, they are a pretty secure system that has been made through the past two decades. Blockchains are primarily used for cryptocurrency, since it has become very popular in recent years. Blockchains can be exploited, but not all of them, most of them are very hard to hack into.

Blockchains Blockchains tend to actually be a lot more than just a secure way of transferring data. Blockchains come in many different forms. Bitcoin is probably the most popular blockchain cryptocurrency. Cryptocurrency is not the best form for transactions though, not everywhere accepts crypto currency. The only way cryptocurrency users were able to send funds was through digital wallet addresses. Each transaction gets recorded as a “block” in the form of a sequence of numbers. This block is now confirmed across networks, while keeping some degree of anonymity across users. Cryptocurrency miners are able to earn coins by organizing these blocks, therefore making these transactions valid across networks. Crypto currencies are meant to be used for virtual coins, and they are usually exchanged on decentralized computer networks between people with virtual wallets. These transactions are recorded publicly on distributed, tamper-proof ledgers known as blockchains. No matter how secure blockchains may seem they are never completely secure. There are new ways of crypto mining these block chains coming out everyday with new methods. The popularity of blockchains have skyrocketed in previous years but people now understand the risk that comes along with this form of currency.
